===Alternative forms===
* {{l|ojp|京}}, {{l|ojp|京都}}

===Etymology===
Originally a compound of {{com|ojp|sort=みやこ|宮|tr1=mi<sub>1</sub>ya|t1=[[palace]]}} and an uncertain {{m|ojp|こ|tr=ko<sub>1</sub>}} referring to a [[place]].
* The ''ko'' is possibly {{m|ojp|処|tr=ko<sub>2</sub>|pos=affix indicating [[location]]}}, according to most dictionaries. However, poems in the ''{{w|Man'yōshū}}'' attest overwhelming phonetic usage of {{IPAfont|⟨ko<sub>1</sub>⟩}} instead of {{IPAfont|⟨ko<sub>2</sub>⟩}}.
* The ''ko'' is likely {{m|ojp|子|tr=-ko<sub>1</sub>|pos=diminuative suffix}}, as some ''Man'yōshū'' poems use the {{lang|ojp|宮子}} reading.
